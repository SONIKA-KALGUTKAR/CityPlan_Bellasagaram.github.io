About Bellasagaram

Bellasagaram, meaning “The Sweet Coastal City,” is inspired by Anakapalli’s heritage, jaggery market, fertile lands, and coastal power. The city blends tradition with modern infrastructure to create a sustainable and smart coastal hub.

Its development model integrates ports, fisheries, solar energy systems, and recycling industries. By using its coastal advantage, the city aims to boost exports, generate employment, and build a green and self-sustaining economy where culture and innovation flourish together.
