About Our City

Bellasagaram is a conceptual smart city designed using ideas from Design and Analysis of Algorithms. The focus is on creating a city that is not only modern and connected, but also sustainable, citizen-friendly, and easy to manage.

Our city model aims to:

  • Create well-planned residential, commercial, and mixed-use zones
  • Use algorithm-based planning to reduce traffic and travel time
  • Preserve green belts, lakes, and public recreational spaces
  • Integrate solar, wind, and other renewable sources for daily energy needs
  • Use technology and data to monitor services like waste, water, and security

City Highlights

Smart Traffic Management

Shortest path and scheduling algorithms are used to design road layouts, signal timings, and public transport routes so that congestion is minimized and travel feels predictable and efficient.

Green Parks & Public Spaces

Parks, walking tracks, play areas, and waterfront zones are placed strategically so that every neighbourhood has access to fresh air, open spaces, and community activities.

Renewable & Reliable Energy

Rooftop solar grids, small wind installations, and efficient street lighting are planned to reduce the carbon footprint while ensuring stable power for homes, offices, and public infrastructure.
