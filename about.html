About the Course & Project

This project is a part of the Design and Analysis of Algorithms (DAA) course under the guidance of Prof. Prakash Hegade, at KLE Technological University.

The aim is to use algorithmic thinking to design Bellasagaram, a sustainable coastal city inspired by Anakapalliâ€™s rich culture, jaggery market, and coastal resources. We connect real-world city planning problems with algorithmic solutions like optimization, graph traversal, and greedy strategies.

Through this project, we explore how algorithms can help in: traffic management, energy distribution, resource allocation, route planning, and building a cleaner and smarter city.
