Project Overview

The Bellasagaram City Design project focuses on creating a sustainable coastal city using algorithmic techniques. We model city components like roads, grids, energy flow, transport, resource distribution, and waste management using core concepts from DAA.

Our design breaks down the city into modules:

  • Transport & Traffic: Shortest path algorithms for optimal routing.
  • Energy Management: Greedy and dynamic programming ideas for efficient usage.
  • Resource Allocation: Optimization-based allocation for water, food, and goods.
  • Waste & Recycling: Graph-based models for collection and processing routes.

Each module is analyzed in terms of time complexity, space complexity, and practical feasibility, showing how theory supports real-world city planning.
