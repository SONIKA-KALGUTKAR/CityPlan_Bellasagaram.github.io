City Design

Our Bellasagaram city design imagines a modern coastal city that is clean, efficient, and people-friendly. The plan focuses on reducing waste, saving energy, improving transportation, and creating safe public spaces for everyone.

Using the ideas from Design and Analysis of Algorithms, we treat the city like a large problem to be solved step by step â€” from traffic flow and resource allocation to emergency services and housing. Each decision is made to balance sustainability, economy, and quality of life.
